Dr. Norman Borlaug: The ‘Father of the Green Revolution’ whose groundbreaking agricultural innovations helped feed billions and transform global food security
Dr. Norman Borlaug (1914-2009) was a visionary agronomist whose tireless efforts revolutionized global agriculture. Known as the ‘Father of the Green Revolution,’ his development of high-yielding, disease-resistant wheat varieties significantly boosted food production and helped alleviate hunger in many developing countries. His pioneering work not only saved countless lives but also laid the foundation for modern agricultural practices. Borlaug’s legacy endures through his profound impact on global food security and his unwavering commitment to ending world hunger
